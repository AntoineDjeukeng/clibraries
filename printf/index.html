printf Flags Logic Table

Flag Descriptions

  • # – Alternate form
  • 0 – Zero-padded
  • - – Left-aligned
  • space – Space before positive numbers
  • + – Always show sign
  • ' – Thousands separator (locale)
  • I – Alternative digits (locale)

Legend

  • ✔ – Supported
  • ✘ – Not supported or ignored
  • ⚠ – Locale-dependent or conditional
